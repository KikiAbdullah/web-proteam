About Us

PT. INKOR BOLA PACIFIC

PT. INKOR BOLA PACIFIC has been the largest sports ball manufacturer and exporter in Indonesia since 1992. Based in East Java, we are fully committed to producing high-quality footballs (soccer balls), basketballs, volleyballs, and futsal balls under our patented trademark, PROTEAM.

We do not just manufacture; we innovate. With an ISO 9001 quality management system and support from the latest technology, every ball we produce meets international performance standards. We are proud to be the official partner of various sports leagues, including the Deteksi Basketball League (DBL), proving that our quality is the choice of professionals.
